PAPPARDELLE WITH MEATBALLS

INGREDIENTS
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 egg
  • Handful fresh flat leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • 100g white flour, type '00'
  • 90ml olive oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely sliced
  • small glass red wine
  • 600g chopped tomatoes, tinned
  • 500g pappardelle
  • 100g Pecorino Cheese, freshly grated, to garnish
  • Salt to taste

STEP BY STEP:


1.In a large bowl, mix the minced beef, egg and parsley together. Season to taste.

2.Take a teaspoonful of the meat mixture and use your hands to roll it into a ball. Dust the ball in the flour and put to one side. Repeat with the rest of the mixture.

3.Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and gently saut√© the onion and chilli until soft. Add the meatballs and fry gently for about 6 minutes until golden brown. Now add the wine and simmer for about 2 minutes until the alcohol has evaporated. Add the chopped tomatoes, season to taste and cook for a further 4 minutes.

4.Meanwhile cook the pasta in a large pan of boiling, salted water until al dente. Drain and add it to the sauce. Mix well.

5.Serve the pasta with the freshly grated Pecorino cheese.
